Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the different subscription tiers available?

    We offer three subscription tiers to choose from: Basic, Premium, and Deluxe.

  • What can I expect to find in the subscription boxes?

    Each subscription tier offers unique contents. In the Basic tier, priced at $50, you will find a guaranteed graded coin from reputable grading companies such as PCGS, NGC, ANACS, or ICG. The Premium tier, priced at $100, includes a graded coin and an additional silver dollar. The Deluxe tier, priced at $200, provides a graded coin, a silver dollar, and a 1 oz. 999 fine silver piece.

  • What are PCGS, NGC, ANACS, and ICG?

    PCGS, NGC, ANACS, and ICG are widely recognized and respected coin grading companies. These organizations specialize in grading and authenticating coins to ensure their quality, protect consumers, and preserve the value of the coins.

  • Who is behind Coin Box Sub?

    Coin Box Sub is a Veteran Owned Small Business based in Texas. The owner, a third-generation coin dealer and a veteran of the US Army, brings extensive experience and knowledge to curate the best coins for our subscribers. With our vast inventory and a strong network of dealers, we are committed to delivering exceptional coins at a great value.

  • What does your buyback guarantee entail?

    Our buyback guarantee assures your satisfaction. If, for any reason, you are unsatisfied with an item in your subscription box, we offer a buyback option. Within one month of receiving your subscription box, we will repurchase the item at 80% of its value. Please note that the buyback guarantee excludes items whose value is solely based on the bullion market, such as 90% silver coins commonly known as "junk silver."
